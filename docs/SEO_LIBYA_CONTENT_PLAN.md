# SEO Libya — 3-Month Content Plan

> Status: **PLANNING ONLY — requires Mohamed's approval before any content is
> written or published.** Prepared 2026-07-05. Companion to
> `SEO_LIBYA_KEYWORD_MAP.md` and `SEO_SERVICE_PAGE_ARCHITECTURE.md`.

## Ground rules

- No thin AI blog spam. Every piece must say something a Libyan
  decision-maker can act on, drawn from how Pattrix actually works
  (message discipline, scrutiny-proof communication, bilingual production).
- Every piece links to exactly one primary service page (its "money page")
  plus one case study. Articles support service pages — not the reverse.
- Arabic articles are written natively in Arabic, not translated.
- Proposed home: `/insights/<slug>` (EN) and `/ar/insights/<slug>` (AR) —
  route naming needs approval alongside service pages.
- Publishing cadence: **1 piece/week for 12 weeks**, alternating EN/AR,
  sequenced so each article lands after its money page exists.
