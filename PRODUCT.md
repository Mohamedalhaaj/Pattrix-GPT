# PRODUCT.md — Pattrix

**What**: Marketing website for Pattrix, a premium Marketing & PR agency in
Tripoli, Libya. Design IS the product here — the site must itself prove the
agency's craft.

**Register**: brand (marketing/campaign surface, not app UI).

**Audience**: brand managers, institutional communication leads, NGO and
corporate decision-makers in Libya and the region; international partners
checking credibility.

**Scene**: a communications director reviews the agency shortlist on a laptop
in a bright office; the site must read as internationally credible in the
first five seconds, in daylight, on any connection.

**Positioning**: "We turn noise into patterns people remember." Strategy,
public relations, and production — from Tripoli, outward.

**Design concept**: the Pattern Field — one generative particle system that
reorganizes per chapter (signal → interference → dark noise interlude → work →
service formations → constellation → radiate → converge). One metaphor, no
mixing. Predominantly light; navy only in the single interlude; Pattrix blue
`#0171DD` is structural.

**Constraints**: no invented facts/stats/testimonials; content centralized in
`content/*.ts`; no raster assets (all visuals procedural); GSAP-only motion;
reduced-motion and no-JS must stay fully readable.

**Canonical docs**: `docs/rebuild/DESIGN_SYSTEM.md`, `MOTION_SYSTEM.md`,
`EDITING_GUIDE.md`, `CONTENT_GAPS.md`.
